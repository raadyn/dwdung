DwDung is a massive multiplayer online game in oldschool RPG style.

You can also try a desktop version of this game: DwDung desktop.

Control

  • Tap on the cells in front of your personage to go stright
  • Tap on the cells left of your personage to turn around
  • Use to say phrase from input box
  • Use to take items from the ground
  • Use to open building's window
  • Use to open craft window
  • Use to show logs
  • Use to forced attack (attack something/somebody which you can't by default)

Inventory

Inventory is on the left side. Equiped items are marked by red border.

Double tap on inventory items show context menu:

  • is for using or equipping items, if possible. Note that you can't equip an item if correspondig place is used. For example, a stick or club requires one free hand, but a pickaxe requires both hand are free.
  • is for dropping items on the ground. If you have more than one item you will be asked, how many items you want to drop.
